Circulatory Health

Fuel your circulation with our vibrant Beetroot and Aronia Juice blend. Packed with essential nutrients and natural nitrates, this delicious juice supports healthy blood flow and vascular function. Rich in antioxidants, it's a nutritious way to nourish your circulatory system from within.

1 medium beetroot (peeled, chopped), 1/2 cup aronia berries (fresh or frozen), 1/2 cup water, 1-2 tbsp honey (optional)

üìù Preparation Instructions

1. Wash, peel, and chop beetroot into small pieces. 2. Place beetroot, aronia berries, and water in blender. 3. Blend until smooth. 4. Taste and add honey if desired, blend briefly. 5. Strain through fine-mesh strainer into glass. 6. Serve immediately or refrigerate up to 3 days. 7. Enjoy.
