2 cloves garlic, 2 inches ginger root, 1 x 2-inch cinnamon stick, 1/2 lemon (wedges), 1.5 cups water, Honey (optional)

1. Peel and crush garlic, set aside 10 minutes. 2. Chop ginger into small pieces. 3. Bring 1.5 cups water to boil. 4. Add ginger, garlic, and cinnamon to boiling water. 5.
