Varicose Veins

Find soothing relief with our Horse Chestnut Cooling Gel, specially formulated for varicose vein care. This refreshing topical treatment combines horse chestnut extract with calming witch hazel and aloe vera to reduce the appearance of spider veins, provide cooling comfort, and support vein health.

üåø Botanical Ingredients

1/4 cup dried horse chestnut seeds or 1/2 cup fresh, 1 cup witch hazel extract, 1/4 cup aloe vera gel, 10-15 drops lavender oil (optional)

üìù Preparation Instructions

1. If using fresh chestnuts, peel and finely chop. If dried, grind into fine powder. 2. Place powder/chopped chestnuts in glass jar. 3. Pour witch hazel over chestnuts until fully submerged. 4. Seal jar, shake well. 5. Store in cool, dark place 2 weeks, shake daily. 6. After 2 weeks, strain through cheesecloth into clean container. 7. In clean bowl, mix strained liquid with aloe vera gel. 8. Add 10-15 drops lavender oil if desired, stir well. 9. Pour into clean glass jar with lid. 10. Store in cool, dry place (fridge optional for cooling effect).
