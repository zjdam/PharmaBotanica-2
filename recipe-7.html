Cholesterol Management

Harness the powerful synergy of two kitchen superfoods with our Garlic and Lemon Tonic. This potent natural remedy combines garlic's renowned cholesterol-lowering properties with lemon's abundant antioxidants to support your cardiovascular health. A daily dose of heart-smart nutrition from nature's pharmacy.

4-5 cloves fresh garlic, 1 organic lemon, 1 tablespoon raw honey (optional), 1 cup filtered water

1. Peel and crush 4-5 garlic cloves, let sit 10 minutes. 2. Wash and grate lemon zest, then juice lemon. 3. Boil 1 cup water, remove from heat, cool 2 minutes. 4.
